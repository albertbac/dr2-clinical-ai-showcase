# Regulatory Exposure Calculator for Crypto P2P Operators

## Summary
A legal-operational calculator that helps assess regulatory exposure and decision paths for crypto P2P activity.

## Primary user
Founder or legal operator

## Secondary user
Compliance or advisory team

## Problem solved
Crypto P2P operators need a structured way to assess regulatory exposure and possible transition paths without relying on ad hoc opinion only.

## Functional workflow
The user provides operational characteristics, the system scores exposure, estimates regulatory direction, and returns structured decision guidance in a public-safe high-level format.

## Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope: exposure assessment, structured outputs, decision framing, and report-oriented presentation.

## High-level integrations
High-level only: document generation, structured storage, and administrative review flows.

## What stays private and why
Scoring logic, legal heuristics, response templates, evidence corpus, and all internal operating assumptions remain private.

## Confidence level
HIGH

## Exposure risk
MEDIUM
