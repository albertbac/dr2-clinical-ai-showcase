# Priority Matrix for Structured Decision Making

## Summary
A lightweight web app for prioritizing issues and initiatives through a structured GUT framework.

## Primary user
Manager or team lead

## Secondary user
Operations analyst

## Problem solved
Teams need a simple but structured way to decide what deserves action first.

## Functional workflow
Users register items, score urgency-gravity-trend, and review the resulting ordering in a persistent workspace.

## Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope: scoring flow, ranking logic at a high level, persistence, and simple operational review.

## High-level integrations
High-level only: identity and database services.

## What stays private and why
Tenant rules, storage details, and implementation specifics of persistence and access control remain private.

HIGH
