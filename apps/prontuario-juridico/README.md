# Legal Dossier and Case Operations Workspace

## Summary
A multi-tenant legal operations workspace for dossiers, documents, calculations, and controlled AI-assisted workflows.

## Primary user
Law firm operator

## Secondary user
Partner or legal analyst

## Problem solved
Legal operations generate fragmented documents, intake flows, calculations, and review tasks that are hard to manage consistently.

## Functional workflow
Teams manage dossiers, incoming documents, structured calculations, and guided knowledge workflows from a single operational surface.

## Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope: dossier-centric operations, document intake, structured calculations, and high-level workflow support.

## High-level integrations
High-level only: identity, storage, classification, and knowledge services.

## What stays private and why
Client matter structure, calculation rules, document schemas, prompts, and operational controls remain private.
