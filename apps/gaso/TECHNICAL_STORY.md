# Technical Story

Clinical Blood Gas Analysis Workspace exists to solve a coordination or analysis problem that becomes expensive when teams rely on scattered tools, manual interpretation, or low-traceability workflows.

## What is publicly safe to say
- the product centers on clinicians need faster structured interpretation of blood gas and related exam material without turning the product into an opaque black box.
- the product workflow is described at a high level
- the public story focuses on delivery, usability, and architectural discipline

## What is intentionally omitted

- private infrastructure details
- copied implementation material
