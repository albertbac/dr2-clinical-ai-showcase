# Clinical Blood Gas Analysis Workspace

## Summary
A clinical analysis workspace for blood gas interpretation and related exam review with deterministic support and controlled AI assistance.

## Primary user
Clinical professional

## Secondary user
Clinical admin or reviewer

## Problem solved
Clinicians need faster structured interpretation of blood gas and related exam material without turning the product into an opaque black box.

## Functional workflow
A user submits or types exam information, the system extracts structured content, applies deterministic calculations, and returns a clinical summary supported by controlled AI assistance.

## Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope: document intake, OCR-assisted extraction, deterministic formula layer, user and admin surfaces, and controlled reporting.

## High-level integrations
High-level only: authentication service, structured storage, and controlled language-model assistance.

## What stays private and why
Clinical rules, internal variable mappings, formula graph details, access-control internals, and operational deployment details remain private.

## Confidence level
HIGH

## Exposure risk
HIGH
