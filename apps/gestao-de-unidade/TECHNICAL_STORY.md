# Technical Story

## Public technical case
Healthcare Unit Operations and Indicator Management exists to solve a coordination or analysis problem that becomes expensive when teams rely on scattered tools, manual interpretation, or low-traceability workflows.

## What is publicly safe to say
- the product centers on healthcare teams often manage indicators, action plans, and operational visibility in scattered tools with low traceability.
- the product workflow is described at a high level
- the public story focuses on delivery, usability, and architectural discipline

## What is intentionally omitted

- private infrastructure details
- copied implementation material
