# Technical Story

## Public technical case
Template-driven Ishikawa Diagram Builder exists to solve a coordination or analysis problem that becomes expensive when teams rely on scattered tools, manual interpretation, or low-traceability workflows.

## What is publicly safe to say
- the product centers on teams often create fishbone diagrams manually, with inconsistent layout and low reuse.
- the product workflow is described at a high level
- the public story focuses on delivery, usability, and architectural discipline

## What is intentionally omitted
- internal logic that would weaken the product if exposed
- internal operating criteria
- private infrastructure details
- copied implementation material
