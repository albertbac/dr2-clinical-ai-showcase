# Privacy Boundary

## What was excluded
- source code
- live records
- copied documents
- private access material
- private infrastructure references
- copied internal playbooks
- copied prompts or workflow rules

## Why it was excluded
This showcase exists for public review without weakening the privacy or security boundary of the real product.

## What stays public
- a reconstructed product description
- a reconstructed architecture summary
- a reconstructed workflow summary
- manual screenshot guidance
- manual metric placeholders

## Why this limit exists
Template-zone definitions, internal assets, storage wiring, and project-host integration details remain private.
