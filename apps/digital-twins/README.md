# Scenario Simulation and Digital Twin Workspace

## Summary
A scenario analysis workspace that uses structured inputs, simulated agents, and layered evidence to explore possible futures.

## Primary user
Strategy or operations team

## Secondary user
Analyst or decision facilitator

## Problem solved
Teams need a way to reason about uncertain scenarios without relying only on static notes or ad hoc discussion.

## Functional workflow
A user frames a scenario, the system structures the context, simulates multiple actors, tracks evolving signals, and returns an analytical output with scenario-oriented evidence.

## Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope: scenario setup, simulation rounds, entity extraction, structured analysis, and decision support.

## High-level integrations
High-level only: news or signal sources, knowledge indexing, and model-driven simulation.

## What stays private and why
Prompting strategy, premium feed configuration, ranking logic, simulation policy, and infrastructure details remain private.

## Confidence level
HIGH

## Exposure risk
MEDIUM
