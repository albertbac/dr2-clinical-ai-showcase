# Seller-side M&A Decision Workspace

## Summary
A phase-based M&A decision workspace that combines memory, document discipline, and agent-assisted reasoning for seller-side work.

## Primary user
M&A advisor or deal team

## Secondary user
Founder or internal transaction lead

## Problem solved
Seller-side M&A work generates fragmented knowledge, document pressure, and high-cost coordination across phases.

## Functional workflow
The workspace follows the deal phase, loads the right memory and evidence layer, asks blocking questions when needed, and helps the team produce reports, memos, and structured decision outputs.

## Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope: phase orchestration, knowledge curation, decision discipline, memory, and report generation.

## High-level integrations
High-level only: knowledge services, report generation, and workflow orchestration.

## What stays private and why
Deal-specific memory, prompts, decision rules, evidence hierarchy, and document-generation internals remain private.

## Confidence level
HIGH

## Exposure risk
MEDIUM
