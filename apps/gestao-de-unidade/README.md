# Healthcare Unit Operations and Indicator Management

## Summary
A web application for healthcare unit management that turns fragmented operational indicators into monitored action, review, and improvement cycles.

## Primary user
Healthcare unit manager

## Secondary user
Quality or operations team

## Problem solved
Healthcare teams often manage indicators, action plans, and operational visibility in scattered tools with low traceability.

## Functional workflow
Operational data feeds dashboards, indicators are recorded and trended, action plans are tracked through PDCA cycles, and teams review progress in a single workspace.

## Public-safe technical scope
Public-safe scope: dashboarding, indicator management, action-plan orchestration, improvement cycles, and operational decision support.

## High-level integrations
High-level only: identity, storage, reporting, and analytics services.

## What stays private and why
Business rules, calculation detail, operational thresholds, client-specific indicators, event payloads, and private infrastructure remain private.

## Confidence level
HIGH

## Exposure risk
MEDIUM
